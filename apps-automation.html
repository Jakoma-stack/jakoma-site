Repurly status

Repurly is in holding while live testing is completed.

Repurly is Jakoma’s LinkedIn-first content workflow app for agencies and multi-brand teams. It is being held back from public launch while the workflow, onboarding, support model and offer fit are validated with a controlled test audience.

Current status: Repurly is not being presented as a live public product yet. Public marketing, app-login and checkout routes are paused until the product has been live-tested and is ready for a clearer launch.

What is being tested

  • Whether the campaign planning workflow is reliable enough for real teams
  • How draft review, approval and publishing queues should work in practice
  • Which onboarding steps are needed before customers are invited in
  • Whether multi-brand workspaces have the right guardrails and support boundaries

Current route

For now, visitors should only register interest or read the holding status. Repurly should not appear as an open app, paid subscription or ready-to-buy product until testing is complete.
