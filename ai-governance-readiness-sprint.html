AI Governance & Readiness Sprint (2–3 weeks)

A governance pack, risk controls and adoption roadmap for safe AI use — built for complex and regulated organisations across public, private and third sector.

🚀 Request a Proposal 📥 Download the Copilot & AI Governance Readiness Checklist

✅ What You Get

This sprint gives you the minimum viable governance and assurance pack needed to deploy Copilot and other AI tools safely — without drowning your teams in bureaucracy.

Governance Pack (ready to adopt)

  • AI policy + acceptable use guidance
  • AI governance operating model (roles, decision rights, escalation)
  • Model/tool register template + minimum documentation standard

Risk & Control Set (practical)

  • Risk approach for priority use cases (templates + decision points)
  • DPIA / AI risk approach for Copilot and AI-enabled workflows
  • Procurement due diligence questions for suppliers and tools

Adoption Roadmap (30/60/90)

  • 30/60/90-day roadmap for rollout and assurance
  • Training and comms recommendations (role-based)
  • Measurement plan (what to track, how to evidence progress)

  • Copilot / AI rollout planned (or already happening)

Organisation types

  • Regulated or complex environments
  • Public, private and third sector
  • Teams needing “safe speed” and clear governance

🗓️ How the Sprint Works

Week 1: Discovery + Current State

  • Stakeholder discovery and priorities
  • Current controls and known risks review
  • Data/content exposure checks (where AI will “see”)

Week 2: Governance Pack + Roadmap

  • Draft and tailor the governance pack
  • Agree decision points, approvals and escalation
  • Build the 30/60/90 adoption roadmap

Week 3 (optional): Socialise + Decide

  • Decision workshop with execs/senior owners
  • Embed changes into BAU (minimum friction)
  • Final sign-off pack and next-step plan

Delivery is timeboxed, template-led, and focused on assets you can actually adopt.
