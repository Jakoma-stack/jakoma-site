Best way to start

For most families, the easiest first step is a short enquiry. Tell us what is happening, then Jakoma will confirm whether an assessment, setup, remote advice or another route is the right fit.

  • Short enquiry first — no long intake form upfront.
  • Payment link only when the next step is clear.
  • Full intake form only after booking, so the visit can be prepared properly.

Service area

  • Standard visits: Banbridge + approximately 30 miles.
  • Outside 30 miles: enquire anyway; a travel supplement or remote advice may be possible.
  • Property / provider projects: wider Northern Ireland considered where the project value makes sense.

Who we help

Jakoma Independent Living is for situations where someone wants to remain at home, but the home needs to work a little better around safety, routines, communication or reassurance.

Families supporting an older relative

For adult children and families who want simple, practical reassurance without buying the wrong devices.

Start with the outcome

  • More confidence at night
  • Clearer communication with family
  • Better routines, reminders and lighting
  • Door, movement, leak or call-for-help alerts where appropriate
  • A setup that people can actually use

Not just for older people

This service can also support people living alone, people recovering after discharge, tenants moving into a new home, families responding to a recent scare, and supported living settings reviewing whether current technology is still fit for purpose.
