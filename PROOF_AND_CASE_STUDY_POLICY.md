# Jakoma Proof, Testimonials and Case Study Policy

Prepared: 14 May 2026

## Current approved proof assets
The public site may now use the following as anonymised case-study snapshots because they are based on feedback supplied by the business owner and are deliberately generalised:

1. AI Governance Triage: "Organisation using AI informally gets a clear 30-day control plan."
2. Independent Living family: "Family worried about an older relative gets simple reassurance setup and written handover."
3. Smart Stay: "Host reduces repeated guest questions with access, Wi-Fi and QR guide setup."
4. Supported Living review: "Provider clarifies devices, alerts, consent and staff workflow."
5. Repurly Founder Pilot: "Consultant turns expertise into a 30-day LinkedIn-led campaign workflow."

## Rules
- Keep case studies anonymised unless explicit written consent is obtained.
- Do not publish names, logos, identifiable details or sensitive circumstances without permission.
- Do not invent metrics, outcomes, testimonials or client details.
- Do not imply clinical, care, emergency-response or safeguarding outcomes.
- Do not claim guaranteed AI compliance, guaranteed safety, guaranteed revenue or guaranteed lead generation.

## Approved wording
Use:
- "Anonymised case-study snapshot"
- "Based on feedback received"
- "Generalised to protect privacy"
- "Practical outputs"

Avoid:
- "Client achieved X%" unless evidenced.
- "Guaranteed outcome"
- "Proven result" unless properly evidenced.
