Jakoma Independent Living

Prepare the home before or after hospital discharge.

We help families set up practical technology when someone is returning home and the home needs to feel safer, calmer and easier to manage.

Buy post-discharge setupWhatsApp

Service area: Banbridge + 30 miles. Non-clinical service. Not an emergency response or medical monitoring service.

When this helps

  • Returning home after a hospital stay
  • Recent fall, scare or loss of confidence
  • Family needs a simple way to stay in touch
  • The home needs better lighting, routines or alerts quickly

What we can set up

  • Communication devices and trusted contacts
  • Lighting routines and simple prompts
  • Buttons, sensors or alerts where appropriate
  • Written instructions and family handover

Not sure where to start?

Tell us the situation in plain language. We will suggest whether assessment, setup, rental-friendly advice or a provider review is the right starting point.
