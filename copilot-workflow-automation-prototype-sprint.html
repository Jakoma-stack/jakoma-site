✅ What You Get

This is a timeboxed build sprint. You walk away with a working prototype, documented controls, and a pilot plan you can take straight into delivery.

Working Prototype

  • 1–2 workflows prototyped end-to-end
  • Clear inputs/outputs and decision points
  • Designed to be reusable (not a one-off demo)

Secure Process Map + Risk Notes

  • Process map with controls and human checks
  • Risk notes: data exposure, access, retention, auditability
  • Minimum viable governance for safe rollout

Pilot Plan + Measurement

  • Pilot plan (who, where, guardrails, success criteria)
  • Adoption & training checklist
  • Measurement plan (time saved, error reduction, quality uplift)

🔍 Examples of Good Prototype Targets

You’ll get the best ROI by choosing workflows that are repetitive, rules-based, and currently depend on lots of manual effort.

Operations

  • Intake triage and routing
  • Standardised document packs and summaries
  • Meeting outputs: actions, decisions, trackers

Risk / Compliance

  • Policy-to-process mapping
  • Evidence pack generation (with human approval)
  • Audit readiness trackers and follow-ups

People / Customer

  • Onboarding packs and knowledge capture
  • Case notes structured into templates
  • FAQs and internal guidance assistants

🗓️ How the 2 Weeks Work

Days 1–3: Discovery + Design

  • Workflow selection and success criteria
  • Data/access boundaries agreed
  • Controls and human review points defined

Days 4–8: Build + Iterate

  • Prototype build and test cycles
  • Process map + risk notes drafted
  • Stakeholder demos to keep it real

Days 9–10: Pilot Plan + Handover

  • Pilot plan and measurement framework
  • Adoption/training checklist
  • Handover pack + next sprint options

This is not a “big transformation programme”. It’s a controlled build sprint designed to prove value quickly — and to do it without creating governance debt.
