# Jakoma Policy and Legal Readiness Review

Prepared: 14 May 2026

## Status
This folder is a policy-hardened public website package for controlled commercial launch. It is not a substitute for solicitor-reviewed contract documents, but it now contains stronger baseline terms, privacy and cookie wording.

## Key protections added or confirmed
- AI governance services are described as practical advisory support, not legal/regulatory advice.
- Home Tech / Independent Living Tech is clearly non-clinical and not emergency response, care, safeguarding or 24/7 monitoring.
- Consumer cancellation wording now recognises distance/online consumer issues, early service start, digital content and fair costs.
- Third-party device/platform limitations are explicit.
- Camera, microphone, sensor and household privacy responsibilities are explicit.
- Analytics cookies are opt-in only.
- Tally and Stripe roles are documented in the Privacy Notice.
- Dedicated AI Triage form is separate from the General Proposal form.
