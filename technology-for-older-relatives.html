Jakoma Independent Living

Help an older relative stay safer and more confident at home.

We support families who want practical, non-intrusive ways to stay connected, reduce worry and make home easier to manage.

Send quick enquiryWhatsApp

Standard home visits: Banbridge + approximately 30 miles. Outside this area? Enquire anyway — remote advice, travel supplements or wider Northern Ireland projects may be possible. Non-clinical service; not emergency response or medical monitoring.

Common concerns

  • Missed calls or messages
  • Night-time movement or doors
  • Falls, reduced confidence or recovery after discharge
  • Too many devices and unclear alerts

What we can set up

Easy contact

Smart speaker or display calling, family contacts and simple routines.

Reassurance alerts

Buttons, sensors and notifications where appropriate.

Lighting and routines

Night lighting, reminders, timers and everyday prompts.

Family handover

Clear explanation of what happens, who sees what, and how to respond.

Start with a short enquiry

Tell us the situation in plain language first. Jakoma will confirm whether a paid assessment, setup, remote advice or quotation is the right next step.
