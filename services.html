Regulated and high-accountability services

Better evidence, modelling and automation for scrutiny and improvement.

Jakoma is well suited to organisations where inspection, quality, safeguarding, governance, service performance or public confidence matter. The offer is not to replace professional judgement. It is to improve the quality, accessibility and usefulness of the evidence people use to make decisions.

  • Convert inconsistent reporting into clearer assurance packs.
  • Model risk and service pressure without overselling certainty.
  • Automate triage, evidence gathering, follow-up and recommendation tracking.
  • Translate operational, technical and governance detail into board-usable narratives.

Good-fit organisations

This language is intentionally broad and does not name specific target bodies. It is designed for:

  • regulators and inspectorates;
  • quality, risk and assurance teams;
  • commissioners and oversight functions;
  • health, care, housing, education and public-service providers;
  • organisations with complex evidence, workflow or reporting problems.

The page should avoid implying that Jakoma has worked with or is endorsed by any named regulator unless that is factually true and permission has been obtained.
