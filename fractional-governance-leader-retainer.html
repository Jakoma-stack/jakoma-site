What You Get

This retainer keeps AI, data and automation governance moving in the real world — not sitting in a folder. You get a steady cadence of oversight, decisions, assurance, and unblock support that helps teams adopt safely and deliver measurable progress.

Monthly Governance Cadence

  • Governance rhythm (monthly cycle with clear actions and owners)

Risk & Assurance Guidance

Delivery Steering & Unblock
