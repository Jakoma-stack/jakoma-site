Fractional Governance Leader Retainer (Monthly)

Ongoing governance cadence, assurance and delivery steering — without the overhead of a full-time hire.

🚀 Request a Proposal 📥 Download the Copilot & AI Governance Readiness Checklist

✅ What You Get

This retainer keeps AI, Copilot and data governance moving in the real world — not sitting in a folder. You get a steady cadence of oversight, decisions, assurance, and unblock support that helps teams adopt safely and deliver measurable progress.

Monthly Governance Cadence

  • Governance rhythm (monthly cycle with clear actions and owners)
  • SteerCo pack (agenda, decisions required, risks, progress updates)
  • Decision tracking and follow-through across stakeholders

Risk & Assurance Guidance

  • Controls guidance (what “good” looks like, minimum viable standards)
  • DPIA / AI risk approach support for priority use cases
  • Audit readiness support (evidence, logs, documentation discipline)

Delivery Steering & Unblock

  • Stakeholder alignment across IT, Security, IG/DPO, HR, Legal and Ops
  • Issue resolution and escalation where accountability is unclear
  • Metrics and accountability for adoption (progress you can evidence)

🔐 Who It’s For

Best fit when…

  • You’re rolling out Copilot/AI and need ongoing governance, not a one-off policy
  • Ownership is split across teams and decisions stall
  • You need consistent assurance without creating a large programme office

Buyers

  • CIO / CTO
  • COO / Transformation Director
  • CDO / Head of Data
  • DPO / IG Lead

Organisation types

  • Regulated or high-risk environments
  • Public, private and third sector
  • Teams that need “safe speed” and dependable decision-making

🗓️ How the Retainer Works

Month 0: Onboarding (one-off)

  • Confirm scope, stakeholders and decision rights
  • Review current controls, risks and key use cases
  • Set a simple governance calendar and reporting format

Monthly Cadence

  • SteerCo / governance meeting + pack
  • Risk and assurance support on priority changes/use cases
  • Unblock delivery and track actions to completion

Quarterly Reset

  • Refresh risk view and roadmap
  • Review adoption metrics and control maturity
  • Agree next quarter priorities and decisions

This is designed to reduce drift: the governance stays live, decisions get made, and controls keep pace with change.
