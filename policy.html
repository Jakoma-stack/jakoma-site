Who we are

Jakoma operates the website at https://www.jakoma.org. If you have a privacy question, you can contact us at support@jakoma.org.

What information we collect

  • Information you submit through forms, including enquiry and proposal forms.
  • Information you send by email.
  • Technical and analytics data where you consent to optional analytics cookies.
  • Basic site security and log data required to keep the website functioning safely.

How we use information

  • To respond to enquiries, proposals and beta signups.
  • To deliver requested information, services or onboarding communications.
  • To understand site usage where analytics consent has been given.
  • To protect the website, prevent abuse and maintain security.

Third-party services

Jakoma may use third-party services to run the site and process enquiries, including form tools, analytics and website hosting services. Where those tools process personal data on our behalf, we expect them to provide appropriate safeguards.

Lawful bases

We typically rely on legitimate interests, contract-related steps before entering into an engagement, compliance obligations, and consent where analytics cookies are optional.

Retention

We keep personal data only for as long as it is reasonably necessary for the purpose it was collected, including follow-up, delivery, record-keeping and legal or regulatory needs.

Your rights

Depending on your location and the applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, erase, restrict or object to certain processing, and in some cases request data portability.

Cookies and analytics

For details about cookie use and how to manage consent, please read the Cookie Policy.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page from time to time to reflect operational, legal or service changes. The latest version will always appear here.
