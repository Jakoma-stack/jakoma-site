When to buy a toolkit and when not to

Buy a toolkit when

You already have internal capacity, you need a faster starting point, and you can adapt and implement the materials yourself.

Do not start with a toolkit when

You need stakeholder alignment, named ownership, decision routes, supplier scrutiny or a tailored governance pack. In that case, the sprint or a proposal-led scope is the better route.
