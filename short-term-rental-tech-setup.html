Smart Stay FAQs

Is this property management?

No. Jakoma helps with practical technology setup and handover. It does not manage bookings, cleaning, guest disputes or platform operations.

Can you help before launch?

Yes. Smart Stay is useful before a property goes live because access, Wi-Fi, guest guidance and protection technology can be planned early.

Are devices included?

No, unless agreed in writing. The package price covers practical setup and guidance; devices, subscriptions and supplier costs are separate.

Do you cover wider Northern Ireland?

Standard local work is Banbridge + approximately 30 miles. Wider NI jobs may be possible where the project value and travel make sense.
