A curated opportunity portfolio for buyers who can commercialise faster than a solo founder.

What the buyer is acquiring

This is not presented as a live SaaS exit. It is a premium, acquisition-ready opportunity portfolio intended to reduce exploration time, improve launch readiness and create multiple monetisation options for the right buyer.

Structured opportunity assets

Twelve packaged concept routes

Each route is framed with positioning, validation logic and launch-oriented materials so a buyer can move faster from idea to offer or pilot.

Buyer-use materials

Commercial rights and handover support

The package is intended to be sold with rights clarity, commercialisation notes and a short transition window rather than as a passive file handover.

Flagship focus

Three strongest routes prioritised

The portfolio is strongest when a buyer starts with the highest-potential routes and uses the remainder as optional expansion or licensing inventory.

Best-fit buyer profiles

  • Agency or product studio that can convert the assets into validation sprints, build packages or client offers.
  • Venture studio or acquisition entrepreneur that wants a faster route into testing and product selection.
  • Educator, cohort operator or premium community brand that can monetise through teaching, licensing or implementation support.
  • Operator with distribution who values time compression and portfolio optionality over starting from a blank page.

Why buy instead of build from scratch

  • Compresses market scanning, packaging and early-stage offer design.
  • Provides multiple angles for monetisation rather than a single bet.
  • Supports service-first, education-first or product-first execution models.
  • Allows a capable buyer to move directly into commercial testing and partner outreach.

The strongest commercial routes

The package is most credible when led by the top three opportunities rather than positioned as a broad template library. These flagship routes create a clearer buyer narrative and a stronger path to recovery of acquisition cost.

Flagship 01

Shopify returns & exchanges profit optimiser

Compelling because the ROI story is straightforward, the category already proves spend, and marketplace-style distribution is commercially attractive.

Flagship 02

UK landlord compliance + MTD workflow manager

Strong because regulation creates urgency, deadlines drive behaviour and the search-and-partner route is relatively easy to explain.

Flagship 03

Short-term rental turnover QA + cleaner coordination

Operational pain is immediate, quality failures are visible, and the pricing model is intuitive for hosts and operators.

How a buyer can monetise in 90 days

  • Service-first: sell validation, pilot design or prototype packages before building software.
  • Education and licensing: turn the materials into a cohort, premium learning offer or internal innovation pack.
  • Productisation: choose one flagship path and build only after pre-sales or strong pilot demand.
  • Portfolio use: repurpose the other kits as future offer inventory, lead magnets or optional client routes.

Price support

A serious ask is most credible when paired with exclusivity, rights clarity and a defined handover period.

  • Target ask: £35,000
  • Best framing: exclusive commercial transfer
  • Ideal addition: short support window and transition call
  • Fallback logic: faster close at a lower number, or partial support retained separately
