👤 About Jakoma

Where ethical innovation meets real-world execution.

🌱 The Meaning Behind the Name

Jakoma is more than a brand. It's a signal — for those who want tools that empower, protect, and respect their journey. The name honours deep roots and personal values: independence, quiet leadership, and unapologetic autonomy.

It reflects a commitment to building things that work for people — not platforms. No vanity metrics. No hype. Just ethical, accessible, and anonymous digital support for real life.

🚀 Why We Exist

Jakoma was founded by a data and digital leader with over two decades of experience across health, social care, and strategy. After building successful systems within government, NHS, and emergency services, the mission became clear: help others build *their own* smart, resilient systems — without giving up privacy, power, or peace of mind.

This space was created to offer stripped-back, human-first alternatives to bloated agencies and buzzword-heavy tech. Whether you're applying for a job, launching a business, or transforming a system, Jakoma brings clarity, capability, and care.

🔐 What We Stand For

  • Privacy-first: We minimise data, never exploit it, and build in consent and control from the start.
  • Real-world usefulness: No fluff. Everything we create solves an actual problem.
  • Autonomy over algorithms: You stay in charge — always. We use AI to support, not replace.
  • Low-key, high-trust: We don’t chase likes. We build long-term solutions that work quietly and well.
