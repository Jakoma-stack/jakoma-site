Founder advantage

Built on lived leadership, not borrowed language.

Jakoma is shaped by more than two decades of work across data, digital, governance and transformation in complex public-service and regulated settings. That experience matters because most organisations do not need another consultant who only knows the tooling layer.

The strength of the offer is the ability to bridge governance, delivery and operational reality at the same time. That creates confidence for stakeholders, audit-facing teams and leaders who need change to stick.

Experience that translates

  • Leadership from frontline to assistant-director level
  • Analytics, data engineering, IG and digital team leadership
  • DPO-level governance and risk decision-making
  • System change, performance frameworks and workforce upskilling
How Jakoma works

Governance-first, practical by design.

The operating principles are simple: make work usable, reduce ambiguity, create assets teams can own, and avoid the kind of consultancy theatre that adds cost without improving outcomes.

Ethical by design

Privacy, accountability and governance are built in early so progress is safe as well as fast.

Practical delivery

The output is meant to be adopted, used and socialised, not left behind in a shared folder.

Product-minded

Where it makes sense, advisory work is strengthened by reusable templates, toolkits and focused product builds.
