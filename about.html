About Jakoma

Jakoma exists to help organisations use AI and data safely — without slowing delivery, losing trust, or creating avoidable risk. This is governance that enables progress, not bureaucracy that blocks it.

Purpose

Most organisations don’t struggle because they lack ideas — they struggle because delivery is fragmented: teams move fast, governance tries to catch up, and risk accumulates quietly. Jakoma’s approach brings delivery and assurance into the same lane.

Principles

Safe-by-design

Governance is built into delivery: roles, decision routes, minimum standards and evidence trails.

Practical artefacts

You get templates, registers, guidance and operating models your teams can actually implement.

Measured outcomes

We define what success looks like and how it will be measured — no “strategy theatre”.

What makes Jakoma different

  • Productised delivery: sprints, retainers and prototypes — clear scope, clear outcomes.
  • Built for scrutiny: designed for regulated environments with audit-ready evidence.
  • Speed without chaos: proportionate controls that enable adoption.
  • Confidential and professional: client details withheld; references available on request.

Who it’s for

Jakoma is designed for leaders in complex environments — public, private and third sector — who need to deliver AI and data capability under scrutiny: privacy, safety, procurement, and public trust.

Executives & Boards

Oversight, assurance routes and decision support — clear reporting, no ambiguity.

Digital & Data Leaders

Operating models, governance cadence and adoption support that stands up to reality.

Programme teams

Templates, controls and prototypes that accelerate delivery while reducing risk.

How we work

1) Fit check

2) Clear recommendation

You get a straight answer: Sprint vs Retainer vs Prototype — plus scope options.

3) Deliver & embed

We ship artefacts and land them with ownership, decision routes, measurement and adoption.
