Built on real leadership and delivery experience

Jakoma is shaped by more than 20 years of leadership across data, digital, governance and transformation in complex public service environments. That foundation matters because most organisations do not need a generic AI advisor. They need someone who understands how accountability, operational delivery, BI, data quality, governance and change all interact in practice.
