Cookie Policy

How Jakoma uses essential cookies and optional analytics cookies, and how visitors can control consent.

Last updated: 6 May 2026

Jakoma uses a small number of technologies to make the website work and, only with your consent, to understand how people use the site.

1. Cookie categories

Strictly necessary cookies and local storage

These are used for basic site operation, security, cookie preference storage and form or navigation behaviour. They do not require consent.

  • jakoma_cookie_consent — stores whether you accepted or rejected optional analytics cookies. Duration: up to 365 days.
  • jakoma_utm_v1 — session storage used to preserve campaign parameters during a visit so forms and outbound links carry useful context. Duration: current browser session.

Optional analytics and measurement

Jakoma may use Google Tag Manager and analytics tools to understand website usage and improve content. These are loaded only after you accept optional analytics cookies. If you reject optional cookies, analytics storage remains denied.

  • Google Tag Manager / Google Analytics — provider: Google. Purpose: analytics and measurement. Duration: depends on Google configuration and browser settings.

2. Your choice

You can accept or reject optional analytics cookies using the banner. Rejecting optional cookies will not stop you from using the website.

You can also clear cookies in your browser settings. If you clear the Jakoma consent cookie, the banner may appear again.

3. Third-party links and forms

Jakoma links to third-party services such as forms, payment, scheduling, social media and messaging tools. Those services may use their own cookies or tracking technologies under their own policies once you visit them.

4. Changes

This policy may be updated if Jakoma changes analytics, forms, advertising or website tools. The cookie list should be reviewed whenever new tracking, form, chat, payment or embedded media tools are added.
